==== 6.5.1.28 FT1-28 Medically Necessary Duplicate Procedure Reason (CWE) 01646

Definition: This field is used to document why the procedure found in link:#ft1-25-procedure-code-cne-00393[_FT1-25 - Procedure Code_] is a duplicate of one ordered/charged previously for the same patient within the same date of service and has been determined to be medically necessary. The reason may be coded or it may be a free text entry. This field is intended to provide financial systems information on who to bill for duplicate procedures.

#### 6.5.2.22 DG1-22 Parent Diagnosis (EI) 02152

Components: &lt;Entity Identifier (ST)> ^ &lt;Namespace ID (IS)> ^ &lt;Universal ID (ST)> ^ &lt;Universal ID Type (ID)>
